ABOUT US

Being value-added distributor, we provide IT security solutions with a full range of services – professional technical support, marketing and sales support, security services and other forms of support. Based in Slovakia, our company is a subject to EU rules, laws and standards, providing users and resellers industry-leading IT security software and services.

Our portfolio consists of reliable solutions from carefully selected vendors designed to protect endpoints, servers, cloud environments, detect and respond to security incidents, provide comprehensive protection of corporate data. Moreover, based on 20+ years of experience we provide a broad scale of IT security high-quality services.
